General Information

Commands

/tempmute [name] [time] [reason] {temporarily mute a player} \ Ex: /tempmute Hidden_Lurker 10m Spam would mute "Hidden_Lurker" for 10 minutes for the reason spam.

/mute [name] [reason] {permanently mute a player} \ Ex: /mute Hidden_Lurker Spam would permanently mute "Hidden_Lurker" for spam (should not be used often).

/unmute [name] {unmutes a player} \ Ex: /unmute Hidden_Lurker would unmute the player "Hidden_Lurker".

/tempban [name] [time] [reason] {temporarily ban a player} \ Ex: /tempban Hidden_Lurker 7h Racism would temporarily ban the player "Hidden_Lurker" for 7 hours for the reason racism.

/ban [name] [reason] {permanently ban a player} \ Ex: /ban Hidden_Lurker Spam would permanently ban the player "Hidden_Lurker" for the reason spam.

/kick [name] [reason] {kicks a player from the server} \ Ex: /kick Hidden_Lurker Mining is not allowed! would kick the player "Hidden_Lurker" for the reason "Mining is not allowed!".

/ticket {view all open tickets} \ Ticket IDs are the yellow numbers in brackets to the left of them!

/ticket view [id] {view more info on a specific ticket} \ Ex: /ticket view 2 would view the ticket #2.

/ticket comment [id] [msg] {comment on a specific ticket to get more info} \ Ex: /ticket comment 2 No you cannot have OP would comment the comment "No you cannot have OP" on ticket #2.

/ticket close [id] [msg] {close a specific ticket with closing remarks -- the answer} \ Ex: /ticket close 2 No you cannot have OP would close ticket #2 with the comment "No you cannot have OP.". All tickets should have a closing comment.

/ticket reopen [id] {reopens a ticket} \ Ex: /ticket reopen 2 would reopen ticket #2.

/ticket assign [id] [player name] {assigns a player to a ticket} \ Ex: /ticket assign 2 Hidden_Lurker would assign Hidden_Lurker to ticket #2. Tickets should not be assigned to other moderators. If a ticket deals with a technical problem assign it to SomethingAwes0me, if a ticket deals with a problem involving the community or something that needs a owners help assign it to Hidden_Lurker.

Ban / Mute Tracking

All bans, kicks and mutes are automatically recorded with your name attached to them on our ban website. You can view all bans, mutes and kicks at reaction-mc-bans/view reaction-mc-bans/view.

Moderator Jurisdiction

Moderators only moderate the chat -- if you view another bannable offense please report it with proof to the apropriate people. You guys / gals cannot deal with any of the following:

  • Hacked clients, xray, nodus, etc
  • Previous actions of players
  • Accepting or denying ban appeals or user applications
  • Major griefing

If you disagree with something another mod did, let us know, not them. Please do not undo stuff that a different mod does, and do not get into a flame war with another mod, or even question something another mod does with them. Bring it up with Hidden_Lurker as he leads the Staff department, but you can also talk to SomethingAwes0me if it's an urgent situation.

Moderator Demotions

Moderators can be demoted for various reasons. Those include, abusing ban powers by banning people who do not deserve a ban, outstepping moderator jurisdiction, banning admins or owners, server defamation, server disrespect, fighting with users, disrespecting users, not remaining unbiased, and loss of trust. Also, if you are offline for 14 consecutive days, without a prior exception, or letting a owner know you may be demoted. You may also be demoted if we think that you aren't a good fit, or aren't contributing enough too the staff team. Please do not worry too much about this, really :).

Questions?

If you have questions ask a Sr Mod, Admin or Owner -- better to be safe than sorry!

Moderator Guidelines

If you prefer to not use the automatic generator, push a branch named gh-pages to your repository to create a page Now starts the important part of this, the actual guidelines. These will tell you a general idea of what to do when someone does something against the rules. These should only be used as a reference guide, always use discretion and common sense.

Note: There will be three parts to each rule infraction, example. There will be the rule they are breaking, an example and what to do on the first/second or third offense. If under what to do, it says [Discrestion], do what you think is right. If it says [Warning] warn the player by telling them what they did wrong, and what will happen if they continue. If it says [Kick] use the kick command, and so on with [Ban] and [Tempban].
